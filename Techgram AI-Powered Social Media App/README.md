# 🚀 TechGram – AI-Powered Social Media Platform

TechGram is an AI-powered content creation platform designed for technology and business professionals. The platform leverages autonomous AI agents to automate research, content generation, news discovery, and knowledge sharing, helping users create high-quality content faster and more efficiently.

![Techgram%20AI-PoweredSocialMediaPlatform.jpg](https://github.com/TheintThinzarAung/Portfolio-Projects/blob/main/Techgram%20AI-Powered%20Social%20Media%20App/Techgram%20AI-PoweredSocialMediaPlatform.jpg))

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Creating high-quality content requires significant time spent researching industry trends, discovering relevant news, identifying useful tools, writing articles, and producing engaging media content.

TechGram addresses this challenge by orchestrating specialized AI agents that collaborate to automate the end-to-end content creation workflow.

---

## ⚡ Solution

TechGram uses a multi-agent architecture where specialized AI agents work together to perform different stages of content production:

* Research trending technology and business topics
* Discover and summarize industry news
* Generate blog articles and social content
* Identify relevant AI tools and resources
* Create podcast-ready content and summaries
* Support end-to-end content publishing workflows

---

## 🏗 Architecture

The platform coordinates multiple AI agents using CrewAI and LangGraph to enable autonomous collaboration across content workflows.

### Core Agents

* 🔍 Research Agent
* 📰 News Summarization Agent
* ✍️ Content Generation Agent
* 🤖 AI Tool Discovery Agent
* 🎙 Podcast Content Agent
* 📢 Publishing Agent
